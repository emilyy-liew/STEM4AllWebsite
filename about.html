About our mission

Our Mission

We make STEM accessible to everyone

With STEM, or Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, on the rise and the evolving job market requiring basic proficiency in technological fields, our mission is to create a comprehensive, accessible, and free robotics curriculum for students with limited exposure to STEM education. While metropolitan regions continue to provide resources for students to enhance critical thinking and probleming solving abilities through STEM education, inequity in educational access has left rural and urban regions behind in this growth. In addition, despite efforts to reduce the gender gap in STEM fields, gender stereotyping throughout primary, secondary, and collegiate educations, as well as other factors, has contributed to women making up just 28% of the workforce in STEM fields. Through the creation of an easily followable curriculum and a mentor network in STEM fields that students would be able to connect with for further guidance, our team hopes to lessen the educational disparity for females and students in educationally underserved communities.
